Services

Treatments

Our dental treatments are painless and fast, with the highest quality results, and without the need for multiple visits. We offer treatment of diseased nerve pulp and treatment of an infected root canal.

Aesthetic dentistry

Smile Makeover: Transformative treatments for a radiant smile. Teeth Whitening: Professional whitening options for a brighter, confident you. Porcelain Veneers: Customized veneers for a natural and flawless appearance. Prices starting from 50 KM.

Fixed prosthetics

Dental Crowns: High-quality crowns for strength and aesthetics. Bridges: Fixed solutions for the replacement of missing teeth. Inlays/Onlays: Restorative options preserving your natural tooth structure. Prices starting from 100 KM.

Mobile prosthetics

Dentures: Custom-fit full and partial dentures for comfort and function. Flexible Partials: Removable partial dentures for a discreet and flexible fit. Prices starting from 250 KM.

Implantology

Dental Implants: Permanent solutions for missing teeth. All-on-4 Treatment: Comprehensive implant-supported full arch restoration. Customized pricing - Contact us for a personalized consultation.

Dental technical laboratory

Digital Impressions: Advanced technology for precise treatment planning. CAD/CAM Restorations: High-quality, precision-engineered dental restorations. Prices starting from 30 KM.
